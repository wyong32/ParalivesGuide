## About Paralives

**Paralives** is a life-simulation game from Paralives Studio, available on [Steam Early Access](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1118520/Paralives/) since May 25, 2026. You create **Parafolks** in **Paramaker**, shape lots in **Build Mode**, then live day-to-day in **Live Mode** in the open town of **Melino**.

The core loop is familiar to life-sim fans — design a household, furnish a home, manage needs and careers, build relationships — but Paralives leans hard into creator-friendly building, deep character creation, and a colourful small-town sandbox without load screens between districts.

Early Access means the game is still growing: some features shipped on day one, others roll out across free updates, and headline items such as pets, seasons, and cars remain on the public roadmap. Saves can survive balance tweaks — name a household you are okay keeping, save often, and trust your installed build over any fan write-up when they disagree.

## What makes Paralives stand out

- **Paramaker depth** — Talents, vibes, stat spreads, genetics, and lifestyles you lock in (or grow) before town life begins.
- **Build Mode flexibility** — Curved, straight, and angled walls; optional grid; split levels; terrain sculpting; pools, roofs, fences; and a colour wheel on most surfaces and furniture.
- **Live Mode simulation** — Six needs, emotions, skills capped at level 20, rabbit-hole jobs, bills in **Paradimes**, shopping, and social systems including **Together Cards**.
- **Melino as a playground** — Cafés, clinics, campus lots, museums, and workplaces spread across four districts — explore on foot or commute via rabbit-hole jobs.
- **Steam Workshop** — In-game mod tools and community packs for furniture, tattoos, and more; patch day is when you re-check what still loads cleanly.
- **Transparent EA roadmap** — The studio publishes what shipped at launch versus what arrives during Early Access; we mirror that on our site and cross-check [paralives.com](https://www.paralives.com/) when lines drift.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Paralives Guide official?**  
No. [paralivesguides.org](https://paralivesguides.org/) is a fan-made resource. For announcements, support, and legal info, use [Paralives.com](https://www.paralives.com/) and [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1118520/Paralives/).

**Where should a new player start?**  
[How to Play](https://paralivesguides.org/how-to-play) for install and your first evening, then the [Build Mode guide](https://paralivesguides.org/guides/paralives-build-mode-beginner-building-guide) if you want a tighter lot-building checklist. Keep the [Wiki](https://paralivesguides.org/wiki) open when a need bar or job question pops up.

**Why do your numbers sometimes disagree with my game?**  
Early Access patches rebalance jobs, bills, and skills. After every update, verify cheats with **HELP** and spot-check wiki figures in your own save.

**My game stutters in Live Mode — what should I try first?**  
Read our [performance guide](https://paralivesguides.org/guides/paralives-lag-fix-performance-optimization): lower presets, check overlays, restart the session, then decide whether you need hardware or a patch.

**Do cheats break saves or achievements?**  
Treat console commands as debug tools. We list risks on the [Cheats](https://paralivesguides.org/cheats) page; use a test household if you are unsure.

**How do I install mods?**  
Subscribe on Steam Workshop with Paralives installed, launch once, then check the in-game mod UI. See [Mods](https://paralivesguides.org/mods) and [Mods & DLC policy (wiki)](https://paralivesguides.org/wiki/modding) for Workshop etiquette and patch-day habits.

**Is the Melino map from the developers?**  
No — it is a community pin map on [paralivesguides.org/map](https://paralivesguides.org/map). Use it to plan outings; confirm lot names in your build.

**What is still missing in Early Access?**  
Pets, seasons, cars, and other headline items the studio lists for later EA phases. Cross-check [paralives.com](https://www.paralives.com/) and your patch notes — not fan timelines — when planning long-term saves.

**Can I use this site on mobile?**  
Yes. [paralivesguides.org](https://paralivesguides.org/) is a responsive web guide; bookmark the pages you revisit mid-save.
