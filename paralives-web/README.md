## About Paralives

**Paralives** is a life-simulation game from Paralives Studio, available on [Steam Early Access](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1118520/Paralives/). You create **Parafolks** in **Paramaker**, shape lots in **Build Mode**, then live day-to-day in **Live Mode** in the town of **Melino**.

Early Access means the game is still growing: some features shipped on day one, others roll out across free updates, and a few headline items (pets, seasons, cars, and more) remain on the public roadmap. Saves can survive balance tweaks — name a household you are okay keeping, save often, and trust your installed build over any fan write-up when they disagree.

---

## What makes Paralives stand out

- **Paramaker depth** — Talents, vibes, stat spreads, genetics, and lifestyles you lock in (or grow) before town life begins.
- **Build Mode flexibility** — Curved walls, split levels, terrain tools, pools, roofs, and a colour wheel aimed at detailed lot-building.
- **Live Mode simulation** — Six needs, emotions, skills capped at level 20, rabbit-hole jobs, bills in **Paradimes**, shopping, and social systems including **Together Cards**.
- **Melino as a playground** — Cafés, clinics, campus lots, museums, and workplaces to explore on foot or by rabbit-hole job.
- **Steam Workshop** — Community mods and furniture packs; patch day is when you re-check what still loads cleanly.
- **Transparent EA roadmap** — The studio publishes what shipped at launch vs what arrives during Early Access; we mirror that on our [Updates](https://paralivesguides.org/updates) page and cross-check [paralives.com](https://www.paralives.com/) when lines drift.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Paralives Guide official?**  
No. [paralivesguides.org](https://paralivesguides.org/) is a fan-made resource. For announcements, support, and legal info, use [Paralives.com](https://www.paralives.com/) and [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1118520/Paralives/).

**Where should a new player start?**  
[How to Play](https://paralivesguides.org/how-to-play) for install and first evening, then [Getting Started](https://paralivesguides.org/guides/getting-started) for a tighter checklist. Keep the [Wiki](https://paralivesguides.org/wiki) open when a need bar or job question pops up.

**Why do your numbers sometimes disagree with my game?**  
Early Access patches rebalance jobs, bills, and skills. We note review dates on pages; after every update, verify cheats with **HELP** and spot-check wiki figures in your own save.

**Do cheats break saves or achievements?**  
Treat console commands as debug tools. We list risks on the [Cheats](https://paralivesguides.org/cheats) page; use a test household if you are unsure.

**How do I install mods?**  
Subscribe on Steam Workshop with Paralives installed, launch once, then check the in-game mod UI. See [Mods](https://paralivesguides.org/mods) and [Mods & DLC policy (wiki)](https://paralivesguides.org/wiki/modding) for Workshop etiquette and patch-day habits.

**Is the Melino map from the developers?**  
No — it is a community pin map on [paralivesguides.org/map](https://paralivesguides.org/map). Use it to plan outings; confirm lot names in your build.

**What is still missing in Early Access?**  
Pets, seasons, cars, and other headline items the studio lists for later EA phases. Our [Updates](https://paralivesguides.org/updates) page tracks what landed vs what is still promised — always cross-check the live roadmap.
